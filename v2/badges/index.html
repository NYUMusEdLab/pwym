Nifty Badges for #PWYM

For each module of Play With Your Music, you can submit your project for a badge. You'll get some feedback from your fellow learners, and a spiffy badge to show off on the interwebs.

To find out more about open badges and the learning theory behind them, check out badges.p2pu.org.
